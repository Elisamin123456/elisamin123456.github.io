This is a Personal Blog created by ELS.

Its goal is to provide any individual, whether respected or disrespected, with an encrypted means to freely share and publish all resources and constructive projects without censorship.

On this website, I will gradually upload the culmination of my life’s work.
Regardless of whether it should have been created.

This will be a long-term, grand project that will continue to evolve endlessly.

Rules:

  1. Show respect to those who deserve it, and withhold respect from those who do not.
  2. Accept that your values are not always correct—only those capable of evolving their thoughts are considered worthy of respect.
  3. Morality is a consensus, not an axiom.
  4. Always be grateful to those who support you.
